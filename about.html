About

Crimson Moon Records is dedicated to making you, sound like you.

Crimson Moon Records was founded by Ryan Chabon as a place for musicians to record their art without limitations. The relaxed and personal atmosphere the studio provides generates unique, creative experiences. Ryan’s skill—combined with quality equipment—provides artists with the tools needed to bring their creation to life.

"gives [the artist] undivided attention." -Christian Wray

Ryan Chabon

Recording engineer & audio specialist

Music has been my passion since I started playing at age 5—starting on piano and then moving to guitar, bass, drums, and saxophone.

I attended the Weaver Academy for the Performing Arts for high school, and enrolled in the highly regarded Music Production program (which produced Grammy® award winning engineer Smith Carlson).

I produce and engineer my own recordings and those of colleagues and produce/record music and soundtracks for theater and film.
