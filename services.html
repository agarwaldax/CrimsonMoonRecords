Services

Recording

Crimson Moon Records provides technical assistance for any recording project or production. This service gives artists a helping hand in capturing the sound they desire. Crimson Moon Records does not have artistic authority to change music or sounds.


Mixing

This is for artists who have pre-recorded tracks and need a professional mix to add polish, balance, and depth. Crimson Moon Records works closely with the artist to ensure the best sound possible. The artist's vision is always the first priority.


Producing

Producing is hands on and involves working with the client during the recording process to assist with arrangement and shaping of a recording into a work of art. This input helps accentuate what makes an artist unique and provides a level of professionalism to the final presentation.
